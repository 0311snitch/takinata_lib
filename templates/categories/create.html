Category Info

What is category?

A category is a set of tasks grouped by one topic or area.

For what?

Categories are designed to group tasks on some basis. For example, the task of "doing homework" and "Buy notebooks" both belong to the project "Study", but to put them in the same semantic group will not be correct. And storage task "mow the lawn" to "redecorate bedroom" in one category of "House" is very strange. But the items "brush your teeth" and" prepare Breakfast "can be safely placed in the category of "Daily routine".

Example of use

- Category "Scientific work" in the project "Study", which will contain tasks that will bring you closer to writing a scientific work at the University.
- Category " Car "in the project "Life", which will contain tasks related to your iron horse, like "change oil", "call for maintenance", etc.
- Category "Daily routine", which will contain all sorts of tasks that people perform every day, like "brush your teeth", walk the dog" and the like.
